Insight Activation Hub

| Builder: Jenny Wu · JennyWuBos@gmail.com
Problem
Research insights are trapped in scattered reports (Word/PDF/PPT), while product teams can't easily find them, trust them, and use them.
Goal
Activate those insights into a queryable, confidence-graded research asset library that drops straight into a PRD, so product decisions are grounded in evidence.
Technical approach
Use an LLM for schema-constrained information extraction from research reports (each insight bound to a source quote to prevent hallucination, confidence set by triangulation across studies, human-in-the-loop review before it lands in the database), layered with RAG semantic retrieval to power AI Q&A, coverage-gap analysis, and one-click copy-to-PRD.
